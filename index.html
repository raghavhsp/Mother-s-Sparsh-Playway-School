Our Activities

Engaging activities that promote holistic development

Tiny Graduates, Big Dreams

We believe every step in a child's journey is a milestone worth celebrating. Our Annual Convocation Ceremony is a cherished event that honors the wonderful growth, curiosity, and accomplishments of our little learners. This special day is more than just a ceremony; it's a heartfelt tribute to the vibrant energy and incredible progress each child has made. We watch with pride as our tiny graduates don their caps and gowns, ready to take their next bold step into a world of greater learning.

Celebrating Creativity & Confidence

We believe in nurturing the spark of creativity and confidence in every child. Our Annual Day is the grandest event of the year, a vibrant spectacle where our little stars step into the spotlight to shine! This much-anticipated celebration is more than just a performance; it's a showcase of imagination, teamwork, and the incredible talents hidden within each child. From joyful songs and graceful dances to charming plays, the stage comes alive with the infectious energy of our young performers. It is a proud moment for us to display the holistic development and boundless potential of our students.

Beyond the Classroom: Festivals, Fun & Memories

we believe that the most powerful lessons are learned through joy and experience. Our vibrant calendar is thoughtfully woven with a tapestry of celebrations, from national holidays and seasonal festivals to cultural and religious events. These celebrations are far more than just fun activities; they are immersive, hands-on learning opportunities. Through the vibrant colors of Holi, the gratitude of Guru Purab, the patriotic spirit of Independence Day, the warmth of Lohri, and the creative exploration of our Summer Camp, children develop a deep appreciation for diversity, culture, and community.

Culture & Values in Action

We believe in teaching timeless values through timeless tales. Our annual staging of the Ramayan is not merely a play; it is a vibrant, immersive learning experience where our tiny tots transform into Lord Ram, the virtuous Sita, the mighty Hanuman, and the wise Lakshman. Through this creative retelling, children don't just memorize lines—they imbibe the core principles of duty, righteousness, courage, devotion, and the ultimate victory of good over evil.

Our Basant Panchami celebration is a beautiful symphony of learning and tradition. The school is adorned in the vibrant hues of yellow, symbolizing energy, optimism, and the blossoming of new life. Children participate in special assemblies, offer prayers to seek the blessings of Maa Saraswati for wisdom and creativity, and engage in a variety of cultural activities.

A Vision for the Future: The Mother's Sparsh Story

How Mother's Sparsh Playway School and Day Care came to be

Founded in 2023

Mother's Sparsh was established in 2023, not merely as another educational institution, but as the realization of a clear and compassionate vision for early childhood education. Our foundation is built upon the belief that every child deserves a nurturing environment that recognizes their individuality and innate potential.

The driving force behind our inception is our Principal, Mrs. Bhawna Mittal, a visionary leader with a unique blend of academic excellence and profound empathy. Her qualifications in Commerce and Business Administration provide a strategic framework for our operations, while her deep affection for children and exceptional skill in managing them form the very heart of our philosophy.The driving force behind our inception is our Principal, Mrs. Bhawna Mittal, a visionary leader with a unique blend of academic excellence and profound empathy. Her qualifications in Commerce and Business Administration provide a strategic framework for our operations, while her deep affection for children and exceptional skill in managing them form the very heart of our philosophy.

From the beginning, Mother's Sparsh was designed to be different. Under Mrs. Mittal's guidance, we developed a unique curriculum that breaks from conventional models, expertly balancing structured learning with the essential freedom of play. This approach ensures that a child’s first formal experience with education is filled with joy, curiosity, and a genuine love for discovery.

Our history, though beginning in 2023, is already defined by a future-focused, growth-oriented mindset. We are dedicated to more than just administration; we are committed to shaping futures. Through regular workshops for parents and teachers, we extend our ecosystem of learning beyond the classroom, fostering a community united by the principles of positive parenting and holistic child development. As Mrs. Mittal says,

“Every child is unique and has their own pace of development. Our role is to provide the right environment and guidance to help them reach their full potential.”

About Our Principal

Meet the guiding force behind our institution

Mrs. Bhawna Mittal

Mrs. Bhawna Mittal is a highly qualified and accomplished educational leader. With a strong academic background in both commerce (B.Com) and business administration (MBA), she brings a strategic and managerial approach to her role as Principal.

She is a dynamic and skilled leader, evidenced by her success as a debate winner in multiple youth festivals. Her core strengths lie in her deep affection for children and her exceptional ability to manage and engage with them, mastering even the most challenging classroom situations.

Under her leadership, Mother's Sparsh has developed a unique curriculum that balances structured learning with free play, ensuring that children develop a love for learning that lasts a lifetime. She regularly conducts workshops for parents and teachers, sharing her expertise on child development and positive parenting techniques.

"Every child is unique and has their own pace of development. Our role is to provide the right environment and guidance to help them reach their full potential," says Mrs. Mittal.

Mrs. Mittal is characterized by a future-focused, growth-oriented mindset that positions her not just as an administrator, but as a visionary dedicated to shaping the future of her students and institution. She is a great manager who effectively combines practical skills with a clear, forward-thinking vision for education.

To contact Mrs. Mittal directly, please send an email or call at +91-9803652046.
